Hardware and Software training

Information technology is a rapidly growing part of today's society. It affects everyone's life in many aspects. Every human endeavor is influenced by information technology and the increasing rate at which what it can perform includes.And ofcourse it is nothing without hardware.Both are parallely important.

We are at National Public Welfare Mission pioneering the growth of hardware and software training. We have been able to set standards by adopting practices based on quality, commitment, and the zeal to make success a habit.

Why To Choose Us

HANDS-ON APPROACH

At National Public Welfare Mission we realize that not all students learn at the same pace. In a classroom of more than eight students, certain students will get left behind because of the lack of communication between the students and the instructor. That’s why National Public Welfare Mission utilizes a hands-on approach in all of our classes. Our student-instructor relationships ensure that no student’s time is wasted. Each student has ample opportunity within the class to get one-on-one instruction and review for any topics covered in the course.

PRACTICAL APPLICATION

At National Public Welfare Mission we understand that people need training because they want better jobs. Because of this, we ask all of our students to bring their real world scenarios into the classrooms. While in class students will work on a project that may actually be utilized by their company. In this way students will be prepared to apply what they learned in our classrooms directly to their work in the field.

OUR CLASSROOMS

We have two simple rules. There is a computer station for each student to use in class and no class will have more than 8 students in it. By following these two rules we can be sure each student has the opportunity to learn in the most efficient, thorough manner.

PLACEMENT

National Public Welfare Mission is tied-up with 100 + MNC's and domestic IT companies where we provide frequent placements.We provide 100% Placement Assurance and Better Salary Packages in IT Industries.
